Gojo Satoru

Gojo Satoru is a prominent character in the popular manga and anime series "Jujutsu Kaisen," created by Gege Akutami. As a special-grade jujutsu sorcerer, he is known for his extraordinary strength, unmatched combat skills, and mastery of cursed techniques. Born into the prestigious Gojo family, Satoru inherited the Limitless Cursed Technique and the Six Eyes, making him one of the most powerful sorcerers in the jujutsu world. He serves as a teacher at Tokyo Metropolitan Curse Technical College, where he mentors young sorcerers, including the series' protagonist, Yuji Itadori. Gojo's charismatic and often playful demeanor masks his deep commitment to protecting humanity from curses and reforming the jujutsu society.
